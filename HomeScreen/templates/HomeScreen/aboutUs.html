About Us

GraphMED is a webservice for displaying medical information in a form of a graph. The data presented in GraphMED is based on the information located in the public database of SemMed.

SemMed contains information collected from vast amount of medical papers, published during several decades. SemMed keeps the data in a causal way, it tracks connections between different terms and builds a database containing all of the connections.

Thus, a natural visualization of the data can be in a graph form. In order to show valuable information, we analyze the information using advanced NLP and big-data techniques to collect the most relevant results. Afterwards we display them in an easy-to-use graph form.
